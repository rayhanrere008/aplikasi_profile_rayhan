About

Hello! I am Rayhan Qalby Ramadhan, a 5th semester student majoring in Information Systems at the National Development University "Veteran" East Java. With a strong educational background in programming and information systems, I have expertise in the Java programming language, Javascript, HTML, CSS, PHP, VisualBasic and UI/UX Design.

During the study period, I was actively involved in making the final project of my course. Through this activity, I succeeded in working with the team to develop a web-based application for ordering online tickets for football matches, a website-based application for ordering crepes online, UI and UX design for the Crepes Juragan MSME. I also have a great interest in the field of Cloud Computing and am currently taking programs from Bangkit Academy led by Google, Tokopedia, Gojek, & Traveloka to deepen my skills.
